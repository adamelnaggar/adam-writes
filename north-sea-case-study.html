North Sea Surf Shots

A surf photography business

See it in action:

The only surf photography business in Scotland. I set up North Sea Surf Shots as an outlet for photographs I was taking from the water around the coastline of Scotland, selling prints to sea-loving locals and photo sessions to surfers. The North Sea Surf Shots brand identity is always friendly and 'stoked', conveying a genuine love for the sea and local coastline.

Setting up the business involved creating an eCommerce website, creating graphics and imagery for the website and campaigns, and writing copy and content in line with the brand guidelines. The content strategy primarily consisted of regularly posting images to social media to reach target customers, supported by blog posts and social media / email marketing campaigns.

North Sea Surf Shots has had coverage in regional press and surfing publications. It has grown a substantial and engaged social following and email list, and created a buzz around surfing in Scotland with surfers and non-surfers alike. It makes steady sales and contributes to Surfers Against Sewage, an environmental charity. Long may it continue!

I used Squarespace to create an eCommerce website, and applied custom HTML5 and CSS3 to fine tune details. I researched and wrote targeted website copy and product descriptions, and created graphics and imagery using the Adobe Creative Suite.

I wrote blog posts designed to be helpful and shareable for the target audience.
